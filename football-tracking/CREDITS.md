To stay within GitHub's file-size limits, the following were **not** committed and must
be obtained from the original repository:

- `models/*.pt` — YOLO weights (see `models/put_models_here.txt`)
- `input_videos/` — sample input footage
- `output_videos/` — generated result videos
- `stubs/*.pkl` — cached tracking stubs

See the included `README.md` (from upstream) for setup instructions.
